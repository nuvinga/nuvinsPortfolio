About Nuvin

Hi, I’m Nuvin. A Software Engineering Student, a Freight Forwarder, and an Entrepreneur.

To give a short introduction about myself, I’m Nuvin Godakanda Arachchi, a 19 year old, reading for my bachelors degree in software engineering. I am also a freight forwarder with hands-on experience in the industry by founding a freight forwarding company in 2019.

Though being a freight forwarder is a job I love, my heart and soul has always been with the computer to develop program and mesmerize everyone around. Simple at start, my parents helped me identify my potential and enthusiasm towards Software, and here i am:)

As a constant learner who’s mesmerized and loves to pursue and study further about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, I’m looking forward to put my heart and soul into any work i do!
